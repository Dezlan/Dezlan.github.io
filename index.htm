Profile

Hi! I’m James Waters, also known by my internet alias “Dezlan”… Or “Dexrend” when the default is taken :D My goal is to become an Interactive Media Development professional. I’m 20 years old and currently living in Thunder Bay, Ontario. I’m originally from Mayfield Kentucky, USA. I’m enrolled in Confederation College’s Multimedia Productions course, or at least i was. The program was revamped after my first year, it became the Interactive Media Development program. When I’m not working in the Mac labs with my trusty headset and Wacom tablet, I’m probably at home researching new technologies or brushing up on some code. I have a solid grasp on most of the prominent programs used in the IMD industry, and I’d like to say that I’m proficient with more than a few. Though I’m skilled at creating three dimensional models, I struggle at creating hand drawn illustrations. I currently work in the following programs. Adobe CC (Photoshop, Flash, Illustrator, INDesign, Bridge, After Effects, Premier, Audition, and Dreamweaver) Autodesk 3DS Max Unity Technology’s Unity Engine PaintTool SAI (for skeleton illustrations, or right into flash for vectors) Celtx (story boards, scripts, treatments) FocusWriter (hobby writing) I focus my skills in these areas. 3D Modeling and Animation Javascript, PHP, HTML, CSS, AS3, general coding. I’m open for project collaborations. If you’re an artist looking for a generalist who can help round out a project you’re working on, count me in. animation of pre-existing character (Flash, 3DS Max into a .gif/.mov) character movement in 2D space (Flash, Unity, Adobe Air/Unity Web deployment) Asset management 8 bit soundtracks 8/16 bit pixel characters (Probably the closest thing to hand drawn characters I can achieve right now.) Video editing and Computer generated effects. 2D and 3D logos and digital illustrations for print or web. Responsive Website Design, hosting, and upkeep.
